Dials

Presets:

Curve: level n→n+1 costs base + (n−1)·step exp. Quest effort: 1 exp ≈ clicksPerExp clicks. Time is active play at clicks/sec (2 = steady tapping) — pure tapping time, not counting water-gated waiting. The board grid scales the hand-tuned diamond so its corners open at board corner level, then caps every inner (non-edge) cell to unlock by inner cells level — so the outer ring stays a slow reward while the interior opens early.
